How to read this registry

Each card reports the stretched-exponential exponent \(D_{\mathrm{eff}}\) from fitting \(R(\lambda)\) on a retention curve. Green values (\(D<1\)) mean the system is on the natural side of the coherence cliff; coral values (\(D>1\)) mean extraction — the channel is being drained faster than natural decay. Amber marks threshold cases (\(D\approx1\)) or inconclusive fits.

Intervention tests (Sweden FTT, China gaming ban, Montréal Protocol, Volcker Rule) report \(D\) before and after the policy change. A confirmed intervention is one where \(D\) crossed from extraction to natural (or back) as predicted. To add a new test, append an object to the TESTS array in this page’s source.
