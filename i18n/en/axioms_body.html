Core Axioms

Minimal assumptions separating what’s measured (invariants) from what’s posited (kernel parameters).

  1. A1 — Existence (MM): a compact, orientable, smooth 10D manifold with fields.
  2. A2 — Projection (kernel): a fixed, non‑invertible finite‑resolution kernel; only invariants are empirical.
  3. A3 — All‑to‑four: all 10 coordinates compress directly into 4D (no global \(U_4\times K_6\) required).
  4. A4 — Constants are projection‑fixed: \(\{c,\hbar,G,\ldots\}\) are kernel parameters in our slice.
  5. A5 — Topology ↔ classes: stable MM topological data label particle/gauge classes.
  6. A6 — Finite resolution ⇒ regimes: below \(\lambda_q\) quantum‑grade invariants; above, classical coarse‑grain.
